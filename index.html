About Me

With over 22+ years of competitive experience spanning state, national, and international badminton events, I have proudly represented my country on the global stage. My career is built on a commitment to excellence and continuous growth, and I am passionate about inspiring and mentoring the next generation of players to reach their full potential.

My Journey

I discovered my passion for badminton at the young age of 6, often attending training sessions alongside my sister. While she eventually shifted her focus to further studies, my coach recognized the potential in me early on and knew it was the perfect time to begin professional training. What started as a fun after-school activity quickly grew into a lifelong passion and, ultimately, my career.
